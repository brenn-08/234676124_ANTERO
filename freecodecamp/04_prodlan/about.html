Welcome to Deja Brew: A Cozy New Cafe in Baguio!

Est. 2024

Nestled in the heart of Baguio, Deja Brew is your new go-to destination for a delightful coffee experience. With a warm and inviting atmosphere, our cafe is designed for everyone—from coffee enthusiasts to those simply looking for a cozy spot to unwind. We proudly serve a curated selection of freshly brewed coffee, artisanal teas, and delectable pastries, all made with love and the finest ingredients.

At Deja Brew, we believe that every sip should evoke a sense of nostalgia and comfort, reminiscent of the rich café culture that Baguio is known for. Our passionate baristas are dedicated to crafting the perfect cup, whether you prefer a classic espresso or a unique seasonal blend.

Join us in our beautifully designed space, where you can catch up with friends, enjoy a quiet moment alone, or immerse yourself in the vibrant local community. Come and experience the charm of Deja Brew—where every visit feels like a warm hug in a cup!
